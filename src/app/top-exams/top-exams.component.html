AP EAMCET 2020 Application Form, Admit Card, Dates, Eligibility, Syllabus and Result

Updated On - October 5th 2019

AP EAMCET is a state-level entrance exam offering admissions into various UG courses offered by colleges in Andhra Pradesh. It is governed by JNTUK (Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University), Kakinada on behalf of Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education (APSCHE). AP EAMCET Application Form 2020 will be available from February 26, 2020(Tentative) at the official portal.


  • Applying candidate should be Indian National or OCI/PIO cardholders.
  • AP EAMCET 2020 will be conducted in online (CBT) mode, having 160 MCQs spread across 3 sections. 
  • The Syllabus will contain various topics from the subjects of Mathematics, Chemistry and Physics.
  • The counselling process and seat allotment is done on the basis of marks scored by the candidate and cut-off set by the colleges. Check Cut Off
  • AP EAMCET Result 2020 will be released on June 4, 2020 (Tentative).

JEE Advanced 2020 Exam Date (Out), Rank List, Cutoff and Counselling

Updated On - September 18th 2019

JEE Advanced 2020 will be conducted on May 17, 2020 in India and abroad. IIT Delhi will be conducting JEE Advanced 2020 under the guidance of Joint Admission Board (JAB). The number of eligible students for JEE Advanced 2020 has been increased. Now 2.5 Lacs candidates will be eligible for JEE Advanced exam instead of 2.4 Lacs.


  • JEE Advanced 2020 is a national-level entrance exam conducted for providing admissions in the engineering programs in IITs. 
  • The seven zonal IITs conduct the exam under the guidance of Joint Admission Board (JAB) for admission into Bachelor’s, Integrated Masters and Dual Degree programs for all the IITs. Know more about JEE Advanced Counselling process. 
  • JEE Advanced 2020 will be conducted by IIT Delhi on May 17, 2020. 
  • JEE Advanced Paper 1 will start at 9.00 A.M. and end at 12 noon (IST). Paper 2 will be conducted from 2.30 P.M. to 5.30 P.M. (IST).
  • The exam will be conducted in the United States (San Francisco) for the first time.
  • To increase student participation, the number of eligible students for JEE Advanced has been increased to 2,50,000. Check the updated eligibility criteria for JEE 
  • As per students’ reactions, Chemistry was the easiest whereas Mathematics was the lengthiest among all the 3 sections.

COMEDK UGET 2020: Exam Date, Registration, Eligibility, Pattern, Syllabus

Updated On - October 1st 2019

COMEDK UGET 2020 will be conducted in an online (computer-based) mode in the month of May. The exam conducted by Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) for admission to B.E/B.Tech/B.Arch courses.


  • COMEDK UGET is a state-level examination. 
  • Intermediate graduates from all over the country are eligible for the exam. 
  • COMEDK UGET Registration will commence tentatively in the third week of January 2020. 
  • Application Fee for the exam expected to be INR 1500 this year.
  • For those who wish to apply for both COMEDK and UniGAUGE, the new fee will be INR 2400. 
  • The exam is expected to be conducted across 300 Exam Centers for engineering programs which offer around 20000 seats.
  • As per the test pattern, the CBT will have MCQs in English only. 
  • Last year, the first rank was bagged by Durbha Aditya (National Public School, Bangalore) who scored 168 marks out of 180. COMEDK Cutoff
  • Only COMEDK qualifiers can check their result and save their scorecard till admission.
