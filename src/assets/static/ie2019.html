The IE virtual education fair is a platform where a student wanting to study abroad can learn more about university application process, funding options, accommodation and campus life! Speak directly with a university delegate from the comfort your home, neighbourhood coffee shop, or office.

Completely Free of Charge

Frequently Asked Questions

What is IE Virtual Fair?
The IE Virtual Fair is just like any other education fair, where you can interact directly with university representatives. BUT, you don’t have to travel to any location for attendance. You can be a part of IE Virtual Education fair online; from home, office or any other place where you have access to a computer/tablet/mobile, and of course, internet.
How do I attend IE Virtual Fair?
It is as easy as 1-2-3. Just register with us on the form provided above. We will get back to you for confirmation, and then send you an access link. We will send you login instructions using which you can attend IE Virtual Education fair on the day of the event.
Why should I attend IE Virtual Fair?
The IE Virtual Fair is a great opportunity to explore your future university’s campus and student life, in-depth application process, specifics of courses, career prospects, and finance options. You can speak to personally university representatives online and raise your concerns and queries with them in real-time.
Do I have to pay money to attend?
The IE Virtual Education Fair is completely free of cost. You need to pay nothing at all. Just make sure you are there on the day of the event!
