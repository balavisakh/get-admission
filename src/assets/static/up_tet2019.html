UPTET 2019 Notification, Exam Date (Out), Registration, Eligibility, Pattern and Syllabus

Updated On - October 18th 2019

UPTET 2019 will be conducted on December 22, 2019. UPBEB has released the notification announcing registration and exam dates. However, Detailed UPTET 2019 Notification will be released at the official website on October 31, 2019. UPBEB conducts UPTET for the recruitment of Primary and Upper Primary Level teachers in various schools of Uttar Pradesh.

Latest: Aspirants will be able to fill UPTET Application Form from November 1 – 20, 2019.
  • Candidates must possess a graduate degree and relevant teacher training diploma to satisfy UPTET Eligibility Criteria
  • The last day to pay the registration fee for the exam is November 21, 2019.
  • UPTET Exam Pattern consists of two papers – Paper I (for primary teachers) and Paper II (for upper primary teachers).
  • UPTET Syllabus includes Child Development and Teaching Methodology, Language 1 and 2, Mathematics, Environmental studies, Science and Social studies.
  • Candidates who successfully register for the test will be issued the admit card on December 12, 2019.
  • Qualifying candidates will be awarded UPTET Eligibility Certificate which is valid for 5 years.

UPTET Eligibility Criteria 2019

UPTET has two sets of applicants - Applicants for Primary Teachers (Classes 1 to 5) and Applicants for Upper Primary Teachers (Classes 6 to 8).

UPTET Eligibility Criteria for both of them are different.

UPTET Qualification for Primary Teachers Bachelor’s degree with minimum 50% marks from recognized university & 2 years Diploma (D.Ed) from N.C.T.E/ Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI). OR
Bachelor’s degree & 2 years B.T.C., C.T. (Nursery)/ Nursery Teacher Training (NTT) OR
Bachelor’s degree & qualification in Special B.T.C. Training OR
Bachelor’s degree with 2 years & BCT Urdu special training in Uttar Pradesh OR
Bachelor’s degree and Diploma in Teaching from Aligarh Muslim University (For Urdu Teacher) or Moallim-E-Urdu Degree before 11-08-1997

UPTET Qualification for Upper Primary Teachers
Bachelor’s degree & B.T.C from National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) approved institute OR
Bachelor’s degree with minimum 50% marks & B.Ed/ B.Ed Special Education from Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI) OR
Intermediate (10+2) with minimum 50% marks & 4 year B.A/ B.S.C.Ed/ B.A.Ed from N.C.T.E./ U.G.C recognized institute OR
Intermediate (10+2) with minimum 50% marks & 4 year Degree (B.El.Ed) in Primary Education OR
Bachelor’s degree with minimum 45% & B.Ed degree

UPTET Age Limit

The age of the applicant must be between 18 Years and 35 Years. Candidates who belong to reserved categories would be given age relaxation as per the norms prescribed by the government.
